About the Museum

Founded by local artisans and storytellers, Willowbrook Museum preserves the craft traditions and cultural histories that shaped our town. Our intimate galleries and hands-on spaces invite visitors of all ages to slow down, look closely, and connect.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the museum fully accessible?

Yes. We offer step-free access, elevators, and accessible restrooms. Ask about quiet kits and wheelchairs at the front desk.

Do I need to book in advance?

Walk-ins are welcome. Timed tickets recommended on weekends and special events.

Can I take photos?

Non-flash photography is allowed for personal use in most galleries. Look for signage where restrictions apply.

Where can I park?

Free two-hour parking is available in the museum lot and surrounding streets.
